Features

Why News+?

In Lebanon, keeping up with the latest news often means downloading multiple apps, which takes up valuable storage space. Given the economic crisis and the challenges people are facing, not everyone has the storage or budget to download multiple news apps. News+ was created to solve this issue by combining the news feeds from multiple channels into one lightweight, easy-to-use app. Now, you can easily select the channels you want, watch live news, and even check the weather—all from one place.
